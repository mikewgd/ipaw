Services

Edward S. Levy is an experienced real estate attorney who has been practicing law since 1979. He is committed to giving expert professional advice and first class service. He is also easy to work with, thorough and efficient. His goal is to respond to telephone calls and emails from clients within 24 hours of receipt.

Quality of advice is paramount, but so is service. Service means establishing at the outset a good working relationship with the client. At the Law Office of Edward S. Levy, each client is kept fully informed of developments with regular updates. Edward establishes relationships with his clients which extend beyond the provisions of his legal services. Many of his clients consider him to be a personal friend and advisor.

Edward S. Levy has handled over 3,000 residential closings in his career. He has handled multimillion dollar commercial closings including the purchase and sale of pharmacies, restaurants, medical practices, and many other types of businesses. He handles all aspects of residential closings, including contract negotiations, resolving all inspection issues, ordering title and survey, interfacing with the lender and doing everything that is necessary to facilitate a smooth and efficient closing. Edward is a very "hands on" attorney who does not delegate important responsibilities to others. He will make sure there are no surprises at closing, and will protect all of your rights.
