On July 1, 1980 Bernard D. Pearl, an honors graduate of Rutgers University Law School, established a law firm to primarily handle personal injury cases from both the plaintiff and defense perspectives. Edward S. Levy, a Mangna Cum Laude graduate of Syracuse University law School, whose practice emphasizes residential and commercial real estate, joined him in 1983. The law partnership of Pearl and Levy was formed in 1987.
